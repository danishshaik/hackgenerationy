What you will learn

In our Hackathon teams have full freedom to hack whatever they want! If it’s a global issue, we want you to solve it! We want our High Schoolers to have completely freedom to explore their interests.

What we will do

Throughout the event we’ll hold workshops run by experts in the field, for anyone interest to attend. We will hold workshops in the following fields:

  • Wearable Technology
  • Behavioral Economics
  • iOS Programming
  • Android Development
  • Web Development
About Hacking Generation Y:

We have a great passion for entrepreneurship, and for showcasing talent among youth. We believe that there are brilliant High Schoolers that are ready to tackle the hardest challenges, and we want to expose their talent in our event.
